01 — Overview

An audiovisual installation — fifteen glowing tubes pulse, drift, and collide, sculpting light and sound into living polyrhythms.

Each six-foot LED tube houses a rising sphere at a unique tempo, triggering a musical note on every period — making polyrhythms visible across space.

A live accompaniment to Phil Kline's Unsilent Night — each tube driven by a Wemos D1 mini and WS2812B LED strip, responding to his score in real time.
